Strategic Narrative Intake Requirements

Moood Studio Agency - Authority-Driven Copywriting

To activate the Strategy Director and produce high-converting, authority-driven narratives, the following minimum context is required. Without these "Inputs," we cannot guarantee a premium, non-generic result.

Our agency utilizes an advanced AI-powered Strategy Director that acts as a Chief Editor and Lead Copywriter. To avoid "generic" output, it requires specific "Ammunition" to build your narrative.

1. The Evidence Repository (Technical Backing)

We don't need polished writing; we need raw facts. Our agent is trained to translate complex data into persuasive business value.

  • Minimum Requirements: Links to clinical studies, PDFs of technical manuals, whitepapers, or internal research notes.
  • Why it matters: Authority is built on proof. If we know the exact improvement in a metric (e.g., "vertebral alignment"), we can build a narrative that is undeniable.

2. The Adversary (Market Context)

Who are we fighting against? Every great story needs a "villain" — the broken status quo.

  • Minimum Requirements: Names of 2-3 direct competitors or common "industry standards" that you consider inferior or broken.
  • Why it matters: To define our unique positioning, we must contrast it with what’s currently failing the user.

3. The Human Portrait (The Audience)

Who is feeling the pain right now? We speak differently to a CEO than to a specialized engineer.

  • Minimum Requirements: A brief description of the person we are speaking to (e.g., "Software engineers with chronic sciatica" or "HR Directors managing $1M in ergonomic injury claims").
  • Why it matters: This dictates the Tone of Voice (ToV) and the emotional resonance of the headers.

4. The Singular Objective (Conversion Goal)

When they finish reading, what is the one—and only one—action they should take?

  • Minimum Requirements: A clear Call to Action (e.g., "Add to Cart," "Download Whitepaper," "Book a Spine Health Audit").
  • Why it matters: A narrative that tries to do everything does nothing. We focus the entire architectural flow toward this single event.

The Premium Mandate

When you provide this "Ammunition Kit," our Strategy Director will:

  1. Diagnose: Separate your core value from the technical jargon.
  2. Research: Cross-reference your proof with global benchmarks.
  3. Write: Produce a Narrative Blueprint designed to convert high-intent users.

Quality Input equals Authority Output.
